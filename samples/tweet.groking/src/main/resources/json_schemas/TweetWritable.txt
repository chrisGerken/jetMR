
Json schema for writable type TweetWritable:

{handle:  <String> , content:  <String> , id:  <String> , retweets:  <int> , isRetweet:  <boolean> , urls:  <String> , hashtags:  <long> , timestamp:  <Date> }


Note: <Date> values are represented as longs (milliseconds)
      <short> and <char> values are represented as integers
